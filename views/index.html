The AI Engine

Intelligence that
learns your strategy.

LienSight's proprietary model is trained on millions of lien transactions and property records. It doesn't just filter — it understands deal quality the way an experienced investor does.

01
Ingest & Normalize

Raw county data arrives in dozens of formats. We parse, normalize, and deduplicate every record against our master property graph.

02
Score & Rank

Each property-lien pair is scored on 40+ signals including equity cushion, redemption probability, neighborhood trajectory, and legal risk.

03
Personalize & Surface

Your strategy profile shapes which deals rise to the top. The model improves with every action you take — clicks, saves, and passes all teach it your preferences.

04
Alert & Act

High-priority matches trigger instant notifications so you can act within the narrow windows that separate winners from late arrivals.
